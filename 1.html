cheese pizza

The most popular cheeses used in the preparation of pizza are mozzarella (accounting for about 30%), provolone, cheddar and Parmesan. Emmental, Romano and ricotta are often used as toppings, and processed pizza cheeses manufactured specifically for pizza are mass-produced.

paneer pizza

For the paneer tikka pizza we marinate the paneer cubes with onion, spices and yogurt. We then let it sit for a minimum of 1 hour and then spread it on a pizza base and bake the pizza. You should also use cubed red/green bell peppers [capsicum] here.
